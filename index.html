About Me

Hi, my name is Jarren! I’m currently the Data Scientist over at DASIL, the Data Analysis and Social Inquiry Lab, where I partake in a variety of fun activities:

  1. I facilitate the collaboration of students and professionals in 20+ faculty- and staff-led social inquiry projects
  2. I deliver data science projects from beginning to end. I specialize in creating exploratory, interactive applications & visualizations for all kinds of audiences.
  3. I provide data-driven leadership and support through contributions in research, presentations, fundraising, and client-facing services

You can find many of my posts and projects scattered throughout the website. My content tends to center around a wide array of topics that include:

  • Public health problems and programs, specifically in Biostatistics
  • E-Sports inclusion, statistics, and theory
  • Advances in statistical programming languages and software packages
